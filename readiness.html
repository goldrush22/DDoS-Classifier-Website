How this assessment works

This questionnaire estimates preparedness against denial attacks. It does not scan your systems. It converts business answers into a readiness profile, identifies likely exposure classes, and recommends priority actions.

Use: complete the questions, generate the assessment, then use the result to prioritise controls or support a business-continuity discussion.
