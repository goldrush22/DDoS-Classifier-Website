    How the game teaches denial attacks

    Conditions

    Players identify which C0-C6 conditions form each attack class.

    Classification

    Players map evidence and condition sets to DoS, DDoS, EDoS, DoW, DDoW and DoA.

    Mitigation

    Players choose defensive controls and receive links back to the mitigation catalogue when wrong.

    The timer starts at 30 seconds and drops toward 10 seconds by round 10. When the timer reaches half of its starting value, the screen enters pressure mode with warning graphics and subtle shake effects.
